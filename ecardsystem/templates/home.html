In some regions, RTC buses have implemented initiatives like the Shakti Scheme in Karnataka, which allows women and transgenders to travel for free in non-premium services, thereby promoting women’s participation in the workforce.

RTC buses provide an affordable and efficient means of transportation for the general public, making commuting easier and more accessible.

The use of RTC buses promotes public transportation, which can help reduce traffic congestion, lower carbon emissions, and contribute to a more sustainable environment2.

Special programs like the Shakti Scheme have shown significant benefits, with over 3.12 crore women availing public transportation facilities within a week of the scheme’s launch.
